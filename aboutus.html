About Us

We Are Ronin

We present a curated database of the best campsites in the vast woods and backcountry of the World Wide Web Wilderness. We increase access to adventure for the public while promoting safe and respectful use of resources. The expert wilderness trekkers on our staff personally verify each campsite to make sure that they are up to our standards. We also present a platform for campers to share reviews on campsites they have visited with each other.

Our Chef

Chef Eddie Warren Jr. Escoffier alumni and US Navy Veteran

Executive chef and Owner Eddie Warren Jr is an Escoffier alumni and US Navy Veteran who was stationed overseas in Japan. It was through his travels throughout Southeast Asia that inspired the concept for Ronin.
