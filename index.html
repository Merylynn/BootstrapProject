Our Concept

Ronin /roe-nin/ noun:

A vagrant samurai without a master

Executive chef and Owner Eddie Warren Jr is an Escoffier alumni and US Navy Veteran who was stationed overseas in Japan. It was through his travels throughout Southeast Asia that inspired the concept for Ronin. You can find pride in Community and culture through the food that locals sell on the streets around the globe. We at Ronin are proud and excited to bring that street food experience to you through our fun and creative menu fusing cuisines from all over. We Are Ronin. Asian inspired…Texas made.
